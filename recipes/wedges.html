Wedges

Delicious comfort food that is quick and easy to make!

This recipie is so delicous and requires such few steps to prepare!.

In this artcile we will cover the ingridents you need to prepare this dish as well as the clear step by step instructions outling how to prepare it.

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Oil.
  • Onion powder.
  • Garlic powder.
  • Paprika.
  • Salt.
  • Pepper.

Steps:
  1. Prehead the oven to 200 degree celcius.
  2. Cut potatoes into thin slices.
  3. Dry potatoes to removes any excess liquids.
  4. Marinate thin sliced potatoes in oil and the powder mentioned above.
  5. Once marinated placed them straight into the oven and let them cook for 20 minutes.
  6. Once they are golden brown remove and let them cook down.
  7. And there you have it, tasty crispy on the outside and soft in the inside, potatoe wedges! Enjoy!
