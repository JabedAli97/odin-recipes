Jacket potatoes

Delicious comfort food that is worth the time it takes to male!

This recipie is so delicous and requires such few steps to prepare and can be enjoyed with so many fillings of your choice.!.

In this artcile we will cover the ingridents you need to prepare this dish as well as the clear step by step instructions outling how to prepare it.

Ingredients:
  • Potatoes
  • Oil.
  • Salt.
  • Pepper.

Steps:
  1. Prehead the oven to 200 degree celcius.
  2. Wash the potatoes and dry them to ensure there is not moisture.
  3. Poke the potatoes with hole using a fork.
  4. Then drizzle them with some oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Once satisfied place them into preheated oven for 50 min and flip them halfway.
  6. Once done they should be golden brown with a crispy outer layer and soft inside.
  7. Let them cool down as they will be piping hot.
  8. Once the tempreture has cooled down feel free to add whatever fillings you please.
  9. And that is it folks! We hope you enjoy this recipe!
