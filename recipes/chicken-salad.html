Chicken salad

A delicous healhy and high protein meal

This recipie is so very simple to prepare and comes with a plethora of health benefits.

In this artcile we will cover the ingridents you need to prepare this dish as well as the clear step by step instructions outling how to prepare it.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken.
  • Oil.
  • Noodles.
  • Kettuce.
  • Tomatoes.
  • Salt.
  • Pepper.

Steps:
  1. Cook chicken in pan with oil and lightly season with salt and pepper.
  2. Allow the chicken to cook until meat is juicy and white inside.
  3. whilst chicken is cooling down boil noodles with boiling hot water.
  4. As Noodles are boiling prepare lettuce and tomatoes by cutting them thin.
  5. Once noodles are boiled, drain them then place them on top of the salad.
  6. Then cube chicken and place them on top of noodles and salad.
  7. Mix everything together in the bowl well. Feel free to add extra oil.
  8. Additional sauces may be mixed in to.
  9. And thats it! Enjoy the delicous recipe!
